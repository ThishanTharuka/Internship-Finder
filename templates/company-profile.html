Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, Google has grown from a simple search engine to a global technology powerhouse. With its mission to "organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful," Google has revolutionized the way people access and interact with information online. Over the years, Google has expanded its offerings beyond search to include a wide range of products and services, including Gmail, Google Maps, YouTube, and the Android operating system. Committed to innovation, Google's vision is to create technology that improves the lives of billions of people worldwide. Google's culture is renowned for its emphasis on creativity, collaboration, and a passion for solving complex problems. Employees are encouraged to think big and challenge the status quo, driving forward-thinking initiatives that shape the future of technology. As a global leader in technology and innovation, Google continues to attract top talent from around the world.
